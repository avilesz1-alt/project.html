 Artistic Overview of A.I Art
Site Author
 Christies Auction House
Abstract
 Compared to the last site, which explained concepts and ideas in a very general manner, this source is far more in-depth in terms of artistic perspective. Alongside another small yet useful definition, it calls to attention the concerns that A.I. art has begun to substitute human creativity in order to cut corners and make it easier to produce works at a faster pace. That said, through the reading, many instances of artists using algorithmic programs are introduced, such as Niceaunties and her work titled 5 Mins To Opening, which sold for an estimated $12,000 to $18,000. For most of the reading, it discusses different artists who have been recognised as a staple in this new world; however, the last two paragraphs bring up the fact that this is just the beginning of something that has yet to be used to its full ability and how the world of art collection is bound to change.
